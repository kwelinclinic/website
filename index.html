Proven TCM complementary treatment method

At Kwelin, we strongly recommend cancer patients to take up TCM treatment to complement western cancer therapy. Armed with 37 years of clinical experience in dealing with Oncology and Internal Medicine, Dr Khoo Cheng Guat will explain to you what the TCM approach encompass, provide you with a detailed analysis of your medical condition and customise a TCM complementary treatment specially for you.

Is TCM for me?

Yes, TCM treatment is suitable for everyone. With a long history of several thousand of years, TCM has withstood the test of time and has been the prevalent method of treatment in China. In Singapore, TCM is also gaining recognition as more and more major hospitals and medical institutions are setting up TCM clinics in their compounds to provide patients with this alternative treatment approach. Call us for an appointment today and let our specialist explain what TCM can do for you.
